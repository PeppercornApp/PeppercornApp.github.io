Your Ultimate Shopping Companion


Meet Peppercorn.

Whether you're shopping for groceries, cooking, planning a meal, or tracking inventory, Peppercorn is the ultimate tool. Here's why.



Shopping Lists

Shop and check off items easily with a simple and clean shopping list interface. Auto categorizing and grouping of items automatically sorts and groups items based on their categories for a faster shopping experience.

Recipes

Add recipes or import them from the web, and add ingredients to your shopping list with a tap. Enjoy a great cooking experience optimized for mobile.

Meal Plan

Plan your week with Meal Plan, and get an overview of your meals with a week view. Easily view the recipes and add ingredients to your list.

Inventory

Keep track of reordering items and stock levels. Automatic Reorder Period allows you to schedule reorder periods for items used regularly. Scan the item's barcode to find it again easily.
